About Me

A New Zealand brand of curated digital greeting cards. A modern approach to an age old tradition, this is a contemporary online platform, designed to elevate the celebration of life's milestones.

A digital space, with an emphasis on tactility, our approach is to partner with thoughtful and progressive creatives, across disciplines, to create high quality designs.

Ever inspired by creatives in fashion, art and architecture, our collection will grow as we continue to collaborate and work with artists and designers who share a similar philosophy.
